Item 1A. Risk Factors
Cybersecurity threats and data breaches could materially adversely affect our business.
Economic uncertainty may adversely impact demand for our cloud services and enterprise products.
Competition in the technology sector could reduce our market share and margins.

Item 7. Management's Discussion and Analysis
Total revenue increased 14% to $168.1 billion driven by strong growth in our Intelligent Cloud segment.
Operating income grew 23% to $83.4 billion reflecting improved efficiency.
Net income was $72.7 billion, an increase from $61.3 billion in the prior year.
Gross margin improved to 69.4% from 68.4% in the prior year.
We generated operating cash flow of $87.6 billion, an increase of 18%.
